ABOUT ME

My name is Mitchell Dodell. I am a Sophomore at Brandeis University studying Computer Science and Business. Born and raised in the 120° landscape that is Arizona, I traded my sunglasses and ever-present air conditioning for a pair of Timbs and a winter coat in my pursuit of education at Brandeis University.

If I can't be found picking out the best theme for my IDE, you can find me finding exploring the many restaurants that line Moody Street in Waltham or playing Overwatch with Brandeis E-Sports team!

WHAT I DO

I'm a Full Stack Developer with a passion for entrepreneurship. When I am not messing around with CSS in the Javascript Console or learning new frameworks, I consult for Israeli start-ups with the TAMID Group.

I'm an organizer for Codestellation, Brandeis's annual 24-hour Friendly Hackathon. If I wasn't busy enough, I am also a Teaching Assistant for Advanced Programming Techniques and a Technology Specialist at Brandeis's Sound and Image Media Studies.
